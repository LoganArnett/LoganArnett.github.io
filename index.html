A Little Bit About Me

My name is Logan and I enjoy hanging out with family, friends and my wiener dogs, solving rubiks cubes, Rx'ing WODs, and of course designing for the web. Everything that follows will be about my life and evolution as a web developer.


This is where I will keep you all up to date about my web development adventures here at The Iron Yard Academy. As part of the inaugural class for the Orlando, FL, location I am taking my first steps towards a new career. This page will contain blog posts, project updates, and of course completed projects as I move through the program and on into my web development career. If you have any questions or would like to get in contact with me about collaborating definitely check out my Contact Info and Social Media pages. I look forward to sharing this journey with my amazing classmates and for all of you to witness us evolve into Web Developers in the center of the buzzing Central Florida Tech Community!
